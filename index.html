My Three Dead Pets

Bruce

Bruce was a red tailed black shark who died from an accidental poisoning. We were given bad instructions from a store owner on how to properly use cleaning solution for his aquarium. The rapid increase in PH levels caused Bruce to suffocate within minutes.

Captain

Captain was a yellow lab who died from congestive heart failure. He had been on medication for the condition. It had likely kept him alive for years, until he eventually became too obese for the medicine to take effect, and he died suddenly.

Tigger

Tigger was a red Tabby cat who struggled with obesity. He died the day after Christmas when a blot clot to the brain caused him to collapse and have a siezure.
