My Dead Pets

Bruce

Bruce was a red tailed black shark who died from accidental poisoning. We were given bad instructions from a store owner on how to properly use cleaning solution for his aquarium. The rapid increase in PH levels caused Bruce to suffocate within minutes.

Captain

Captain was a yellow lab who died from congestive heart failure. He had been on medication for his condition - it had likely kept him alive for years, until eventually he became too obese for the medicine to take effect, and he died suddenly.

Tigger

Tigger was a red Tabby cat who tended to struggle with obesity. He died the day after Christmas when a blot clot to the brain caused him to collapse and have a siezure.
